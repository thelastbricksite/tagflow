What is a public IP?

It's how the internet identifies your network — assigned by your ISP, shared by all devices at your location.

🔒

Is my IP sensitive?

Your IP can reveal your ISP and approximate city. Use a VPN to mask it from websites you visit.

🔄

Does it change?

Most home IPs are dynamic — they change when your router restarts or periodically per ISP policy.

🌐

IPv4 vs IPv6

IPv4 is the classic 4-part address. IPv6 is the newer longer format — not all connections show both.
