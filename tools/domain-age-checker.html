Why domain age matters

Older domains often have more SEO trust. New domains take time to build authority.

⚠️

Expiry risk

If a domain expires without renewal, anyone can register it. Check regularly if you own one.

🔒

Privacy protected?

Many owners use WHOIS privacy services — registrant details may show the registrar instead.

🌐

WHOIS limitations

Some TLDs restrict public WHOIS data (e.g. .eu, .uk). Results may be partial for these.
